Popular Indian Competitive Exams

  • UPSC Civil Services

    The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) conducts the Civil Services Examination...

  • JEE (Joint Entrance Examination)

    JEE is an entrance exam for admission to top engineering colleges in India...

  • NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test)

    NEET is the entrance exam for admission to medical and dental courses...

  • CAT (Common Admission Test)

    CAT is a management aptitude test for admission to top B-Schools in India...

  • GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering)

    GATE is an exam for admission to postgraduate engineering programs and PSU jobs...

  • SSC CGL (Staff Selection Commission Combined Graduate Level)

    SSC CGL is conducted for recruitment to various Group B and Group C posts...

  • IBPS PO (Institute of Banking Personnel Selection Probationary Officer)

    IBPS PO exam is for recruitment of Probationary Officers in various banks...

  • UGC NET (University Grants Commission National Eligibility Test)

    UGC NET is conducted for determining the eligibility of candidates for lectureship...

  • NDA (National Defence Academy) Exam

    NDA exam is for admission to the Indian Army, Navy, and Air Force...

  • CLAT (Common Law Admission Test)

    CLAT is an entrance exam for admission to undergraduate and postgraduate law programs...
