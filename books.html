Recommended Books for Competitive Exams

UPSC Civil Services Examination

  • "Indian Polity" by M. Laxmikanth
  • "Indian Economy" by Ramesh Singh
  • "A Brief History of Modern India" by Spectrum
  • "Certificate Physical and Human Geography" by Goh Cheng Leong
  • "General Studies Paper I Manual" by MHE

SSC Combined Graduate Level (CGL) Examination

  • "Quantitative Aptitude for Competitive Examinations" by R.S. Aggarwal
  • "Fast Track Objective Arithmetic" by Rajesh Verma
  • "English for General Competitions" by Plinth to Paramount
  • "General Knowledge" by Lucent
  • "Analytical Reasoning" by M.K. Pandey

Banking Exams (IBPS PO, SBI PO, etc.)

  • "Quantitative Aptitude for Competitive Examinations" by R.S. Aggarwal
  • "A Modern Approach to Verbal & Non-Verbal Reasoning" by R.S. Aggarwal
  • "Banking Awareness" by Arihant Experts
  • "Objective General English" by S.P. Bakshi
  • "Data Interpretation & Data Sufficiency" by Kiran Publication

GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering)

  • Books related to your specific engineering discipline
  • "GATE Guide" by GKP Publications
  • "GATE Tutor" by Arihant Experts
  • "Engineering Mathematics" by B.S. Grewal
  • Previous years' GATE papers and solved question papers

CAT (Common Admission Test)

  • "How to Prepare for Quantitative Aptitude for the CAT" by Arun Sharma
  • "How to Prepare for Verbal Ability and Reading Comprehension for the CAT" by Arun Sharma and Meenakshi Upadhyay
  • "Logical Reasoning and Data Interpretation for the CAT" by Nishit Sinha
  • "Word Power Made Easy" by Norman Lewis
  • "The Pearson Guide to Data Interpretation and Logical Reasoning" by Nishit K. Sinha

JEE (Joint Entrance Examination)

  • Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics textbooks of Class 11 and 12 (NCERT)
  • "Concepts of Physics" by H.C. Verma
  • "Organic Chemistry" by Morrison and Boyd
  • "Mathematics for Joint Entrance Examination JEE (Advanced): Algebra" by G.Tewani
  • Previous years' JEE papers and mock tests

NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test)

  • Physics, Chemistry, and Biology textbooks of Class 11 and 12 (NCERT)
  • "Objective NCERT at your Fingertips for NEET-AIIMS - Physics, Chemistry, and Biology" by MTG Editorial Board
  • "Trueman's Elementary Biology" by K.N. Bhatia and M.P. Tyagi
  • "Modern ABC of Chemistry" by S.P. Jauhar
  • Previous years' NEET papers and mock tests

NDA (National Defence Academy) Entrance Exam

  • "Pathfinder for NDA & NA" by Arihant Experts
  • "Chapterwise-Sectionwise Solved Papers NDA & NA" by Arihant Experts
  • "Mathematics for NDA and NA" by R.S. Aggarwal
  • "Objective General English" by S.P. Bakshi
  • "History of Modern India" by Bipan Chandra

UGC NET (National Eligibility Test)

  • Subject-specific books related to your field of study
  • "UGC NET/JRF/SLET General Paper-1 Teaching & Research Aptitude" by Arihant Experts
  • "Trueman's UGC NET/SET General Paper I" by M. Gagan and Sajit Kumar
  • Previous years' question papers and study materials from reputed coaching institutes

  • "Word Power Made Easy" by Norman Lewis
